Last Updated: April 18, 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy ("Policy") explains how Halit Tokmak ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with our mobile games ("Apps"). By downloading or using our games, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Advertising Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (AdMob mediation, Unity Ads), IDFA/IDFV (Meta Audience Network)
  • Analytics Data: Session duration, levels played, in‑game behavior (via Firebase Analytics, Unity Analytics, Yandex AppMetrica)
  • In‑App Purchase Events: Purchase confirmation and transaction status (via Unity IAP)
  • Meta SDK: Advertising ID and in-game events for attribution and CPI/CPA campaign optimization
  • No Other Personal Data: We do not collect names, emails, contacts, photos, files, or any sensitive personal information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To serve personalized ads via AdMob, Unity Ads, Meta Audience Network
  • To measure and optimize user acquisition campaigns (CPI/CPA)
  • To improve game features and level balancing through analytics
  • To validate in‑app purchases and prevent fraud
